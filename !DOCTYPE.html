About ASSERT

ASSERT was established in 2012 as an international exchange platform for Asian students majoring in educational engineering. The initiators are Professor Kenichi Kubota from Kansai University (Japan), Professor Sungho Kwon from Hanyang University (Korea), Professor Chen Li from Beijing Normal University and Professor Li Kedong of South China Normal University respectively.

ASSERT2025 will be held at Chonnam National University in South Korea under the theme "Unfolding Vision of Education with Emerging Technology".

The evolution of technology used in the educational sector is relentless. Even looking at the last decade, new technologies such as VR, AR, and generative AI have been introduced one after another, with various applications being explored. This indicates not just the utilization of technology but a shift in the way education and learning are approached. This shift is characterized by terms such as individualization, optimization, acceleration, self-paced learning, and self-regulation. At ASSERT 2025, let us consider how to interpret these trends and what approaches to adopt as educators.
