About ASSERT

ASSERT was established in 2012 as an international exchange platform for Asian students majoring in educational technology. The 2025 conference will focus on "Transforming Future Education through AI+X Innovation".

ASSERT 2025 is the Asian Students' SEminar and Round Table. This community is an academic seminar launched in 2012 as an international exchange platform to foster emerging scholars in educational technology from China, Japan, and Korea. Over the past two decades, ASSERT has served as a unique venue where graduate students and early-career researchers can share insights, exchange ideas, and build a vibrant academic community across East Asia.

The conference welcomes research presentations in the field of educational technology, including topics related to performative approaches. The program will feature presentations by students and researchers, along with keynote speeches and workshops to foster academic exchange and collaboration.
