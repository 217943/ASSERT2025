About ASSERT

ASSERT 2025 is the Asian Students' SEminar and Round Table. This community is an academic seminar launched in 2012 as an international exchange platform to foster emerging scholars in educational technology from China, Japan, and Korea.

Over the past two decades, ASSERT has served as a unique venue where graduate students and early-career researchers can share insights, exchange ideas, and build a vibrant academic community across East Asia.

Theme of ASSERT 2025:

"Transforming Future Education through AI+X Innovation"

We welcome research presentations in the field of educational technology, including topics related to performative approaches. The program will feature presentations by students and researchers, along with keynote speeches and workshops to foster academic exchange and collaboration.

ASSERT History

2012

ASSERT was established as an international exchange platform for Asian students majoring in educational engineering.

2013-2019

Annual conferences were held in rotation among universities in China, Japan, and Korea.

2020-2022

Virtual conferences during the global pandemic period.

2023-2024

Return to in-person conferences with expanded participation.

2025

ASSERT hosted by Chonnam National University in South Korea, focusing on AI+X Innovation in education.
